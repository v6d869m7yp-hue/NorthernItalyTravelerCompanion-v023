Your two days in Milan

Brera, Duomo, and Essential Milan

You arrive September 9 at 3:00 PM and depart September 11 at 6:40 AM. Your two-day itinerary focuses on Milan's cultural heart: the Brera neighborhood, the Gothic Duomo, Castello Sforzesco, Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II, and La Scala.

Day One: Brera and Evening

Afternoon (after 3 PM check-in): Rest at Urban Hive Milano. Late Afternoon/Evening: Explore Brera's galleries, antique shops, and narrow streets. Stroll Via Brera and via Fiori Chiari. The neighborhood is within walking distance of your hotel, making evening aperitivo and dinner particularly convenient. Choose a neighborhood trattoria for risotto alla milanese or modern Italian cuisine. Return early for rest.

Day Two: Duomo, Scala, and Departure Prep

Morning: Visit the Duomo di Milano—the iconic Gothic cathedral. Climb to the rooftop for panoramic views, or explore the interior. Afternoon: Walk through Galleria Vittorio Emanuele II (the famous glass arcade) and the La Scala neighborhood. Lunch or aperitivo at a café. Evening: Early dinner near Urban Hive Milano. Return by 8 PM to prepare for your 4:30 AM departure.
